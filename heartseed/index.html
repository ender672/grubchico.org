Heartseed Farm is a small and young farm on the south end of Chico.

Market Every Wednesday

From 4pm to 7pm at 1525 Dayton Rd.

We invite you to visit the farm and purchase fresh produce and other items. Other CSA's have farm pick-up so we've gone a step further and set up a market place every Wednesday.

Practices

We are growing an abundance of fruits and vegetables, and we are doing it while improving the soil on our farm. Most of our row crops are grown on raised beds, and after we finish harvesting a crop, we simply add more compost on top of the bed and plant again.

Farmer's Market

We are proud to be at the Chapmantown Friday Farmer's Market. From 2pm to 5:30pm in the Park off Cleveland St. Support this small market that features live music, a playground for children and of course fresh produce from local farmers.

Join Our CSA

Pick-up is Wednesday at the farm. The cost $100/month with a start up cost of $120 that can be spread out in payments. Your weekly box is a selection of what is in season; veggies, fruit, herbs, cut flowers & eggs.
