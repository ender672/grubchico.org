--- layout: post title: Community Seed Swap, Oct. 9th date: 2012-10-06 ---

Tuesday October 9th, 4:00pm to 7:00pm GRUB Cooperative 1525 Dayton Rd. Chico, CA.

What’s provided:

  • Education and information on tables throughout the event about:
    • Open pollinated, heirloom and hybrid seed as well as saving seed
    • Seed Freedom Fortnight and global seed awareness
    • GMO’s, seed sovereignty, and the Right to Know campaign
  • Seeds provided in ‘potluck’ style exchange and seeds for purchase*
  • Plants and snacks for sale -GRUB Grown plants, local snacks

What to bring:

  • C h i l d r e n w e l c o m e !
  • Seeds, bulbs, plants, cuttings, surplus garden harvest and more to
    exchange -please label your seeds or plants
  • Used envelopes or other containers, pens/pencils -for your new seeds!
  • Open heart & mind -as a community we can acclimatize local seed,
    preserve our seed heritage and promote biodiversity!

YOU DO NOT NEED TO HAVE SEEDS IN ORDER TO ATTEND!!
