--- layout: post title: Native Grass Planting & Vegetable Farming Day date: 2010-03-09 ---

This week we will be planting native grass in place of our non-native grass. We selected a part shade, native grass from Hedge Row Farms.

We will also be weeding some of our onions, garlic and artichokes. We will be using wheel hoes, hula hoes, and hand weeding.

If you are interested in volunteering or if you just want to hang out and learn, please join us:

Where: 1525 Dayton Rd. When: Sunday, March 14th, 10am - 12pm
