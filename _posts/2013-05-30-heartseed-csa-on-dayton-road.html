--- layout: post title: Heartseed CSA on Dayton Road date: 2013-05-30 ---

Heartseed CSA is now accepting members.

The GRUB CSA has moved to a new farm on West Sacramento Avenue.

Meanwhile, Max, Elliott, and Ron have been busy growing vegetables and fruit at the Dayton Road Farm.

This new farm is called Heartseed Farm.

Weekly boxes will be filled with a diverse seasonal selection of fruit, vegetables, herbs and eggs.

Pork grown at Dayton Road is also available on request.

You can find our produce at Chico Natural Foods Cooperative.

For more information, contact heartseed@grubchico.org.
