--- layout: post title: Institutional Stove Demonstration on August 8th date: 2011-08-07 ---

5pm at the GRUB Cooperative, 1525 Dayton Rd.

The Institutional Stove Project at the Aprovecho Research Center is arranging a demonstration in Chico of the 60 Liter Stove. The 60 Liter Stove is used for both cooking and sterilization of medical equipment using an autoclave. In addition, the associated equipment such as a briquette making press for making non-wood fuel for the stove will be presented as well.

For any questions, contact Tristan Reaper, (805) 455-8730.
