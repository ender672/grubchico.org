--- layout: post title: Movie - The World According to Monsanto, September 12 date: 2011-08-31 ---

Monday, September 12, 7:30pm to 10pm at the GRUB Cooperative, 1525 Dayton Rd.

The GRUB Cooperative will be showing The World According to Monsanto to help bring awareness to the GMO Labeling campaign in California. Donations accepted for snacks and drinks will go toward helping the local GMO campaign. Please ride your bike if you can and bring a cozy blanket or chair to sit under the stars. Visit labelgmos.org for more info.
